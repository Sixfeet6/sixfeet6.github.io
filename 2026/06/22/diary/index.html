助跑跑姿练习

跑姿练习只能在低速状态下进行, 但目的是在高速状态下能够尽可能 保持动作不变性, 以维持上板起跳的稳定性.

倒数第二步

倒数第二步是 “踢小腿+扒地“ 的动作. 为了降低重心, 要将小腿适度前踢, 结合扒地动作, 减少速度损失.

AI和计算机在研究中的作用

Mathematical research once relied heavily on human instinct and calculation, but computers and AI have transformed this approach by deepening our insights. For instance, I recently used a determinant visualization program that greatly simplifies the workflow. It identifies the exact variables contained within each term and presents them clearly in a matrix-based image.
