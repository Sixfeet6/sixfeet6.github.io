Unit 2 单词集锦 (Cinema Dialogue Edition)

🎬 Unit 2 单词集锦 (Dialogue Edition)

丢掉沉重的背景，只留下那些充满电影张力的瞬间。:)

  1. uncanny [ʌnˈkæni]

    • 释义: 神秘的，不可思议的 (Strange or mysterious)
    • 台词: “He has an uncanny ability to know exactly when I’m lying. It’s like he can hear my pulse.”
    • 翻译: “他有一种不可思议的能力，能准确知道我什么时候在撒谎。简直就像他能听到我的脉搏一样。”

  2. quattrocento [ˌkwɑːtrəˈtʃentoʊ]

    • 释义: 15世纪的意大利艺术 (15th century Italian art)
    • 台词: “This isn’t just a painting; it’s a quattrocento masterpiece stolen from a gallery in Rome. Do you have any idea who’s looking for this?”
    • 翻译: “这不只是一幅画；这是从罗马画廊偷来的15世纪大师杰作。你知道有多少人在找这玩意儿吗？”

  3. sfumato [sfuːˈmɑːtoʊ]

    • 释义: 晕涂法 (Blending colors subtly)
    • 台词: “Use sfumato on the edges. Make it look like a dream, or a memory that’s starting to fade.”
    • 翻译: “在边缘用晕涂法。让它看起来像场梦，或者一段开始褪色的记忆。”

  4. mastery [ˈmæstəri]

    • 释义: 精通，掌控 (Great skill or power)
    • 台词: “You think you have mastery over this city? You’re just a tenant, and rent is due.”
    • 翻译: “你以为你掌控了这座城市？你只是个租客，而且该交租了。”

  5. counterbalance [ˈkaʊntərbæləns]

    • 释义: 抗衡，抵消 (A balancing force)
    • 台词: “Every move you make has a counterbalance. You kill one of mine, I kill two of yours. That’s the math.”
    • 翻译: “你的每一步行动都有代价。你杀我一个，我杀你两个。这就是规矩。”

  6. painstaking [ˈpeɪnzteɪkɪŋ]

    • 释义: 刻苦的，细心的 (Very careful)
    • 台词: “He spent years on a painstaking plan to disappear. And you found him in a week? I’m impressed.”
    • 翻译: “他花了数年时间精心地计划消失。而你一周就找到他了？我挺佩服的。”

  7. elude [iˈluːd]

    • 释义: 逃避，使……迷惑 (To escape; to fail to grasp)
    • 台词: “The truth will always elude you because you’re looking for answers where there are only secrets.”
    • 翻译: “真相永远会让你扑空，因为你在只有秘密的地方寻找答案。”

  8. indistinct [ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋkt]

    • 释义: 模糊的，不清楚的 (Not clear)
    • 台词: “The security footage is too indistinct. I can see a figure, but I can’t see a face.”
    • 翻译: “监控录像太模糊了。我能看到一个身影，但看不清脸。”

  9. transcend [trænˈsend]

    • 释义: 超越 (To rise above)
    • 台词: “To win this war, we have to transcend our humanity. We have to become the monsters they’re afraid of.”
    • 翻译: “为了赢下这场战争，我们必须超越人性。我们必须变成他们所恐惧的那种怪物。”

  10. mysticism [ˈmɪstɪsɪzəm]

    • 释义: 神秘主义 (Attaining union with the divine)
    • 台词: “Don’t talk to me about mysticism. I deal in cold, hard cash and warm, fresh blood.”
    • 翻译: “别跟我扯什么神秘主义。我只跟冰冷的现金和温热的鲜血打交道。”

  11. elixir [iˈlɪksər]

    • 释义: 灵药，万能药 (A magical potion)
    • 台词: “Power is the ultimate elixir. Once you taste it, nothing else satisfies.”
    • 翻译: “权力是终极的灵药。一旦尝过滋味，其他任何东西都无法让你满足。”

  12. indigenous [ɪnˈdʒenəs]

    • 释义: 本土的 (Native)
    • 台词: “We are the indigenous inhabitants of this darkness. You’re just a tourist with a flashlight.”
    • 翻译: “我们是这片黑暗的原住民。你只是个拿着手电筒的游客。”

  13. cornerstone [ˈkɔːrnərstoʊn]

    • 释义: 奠基石，核心 (A fundamental basis)
    • 台词: “Fear is the cornerstone of any empire. Without it, the people forget who owns the throne.”
    • 翻译: “恐惧是任何帝国的基石。没有它，人们就会忘记谁才是宝座的主人。”

  14. seclusion [sɪˈkluːʒn]

    • 释义: 隐居，隔绝 (State of being private)
    • 台词: “After what happened, I thought you’d prefer seclusion. But here you are, back in the lions’ den.”
    • 翻译: “在那件事发生后，我还以为你会喜欢隐居。但你还是回来了，回到了这个龙潭虎穴。”

  15. pervade [pərˈveɪd]

    • 释义: 弥漫，遍及 (To spread through)
    • 台词: “The smell of rot pervades the entire building. Something died here a long time ago.”
    • 翻译: “腐烂的味道弥漫了整栋建筑。很久以前有什么东西死在了这里。”

  16. unassertive [ˌʌnəˈsɜːrtɪv]

    • 释义: 谦逊的，不爱表现的 (Modest; shy)
    • 台词: “He was so unassertive that no one noticed when he walked out of the vault with ten million dollars.”
    • 翻译: “他表现得如此低调不起眼，以至于他带着一千万美元走出保险库时，都没人注意到。”

  17. literati [ˌlɪtəˈrɑːti]

    • 释义: 文人，学者 (Educated people)
    • 台词: “The literati will write poems about this revolution, but we’re the ones who have to bleed for it.”
    • 翻译: “那些文人学者会为这场革命写诗，但我们才是得为此流血牺牲的人。”

  18. superfluous [suːˈpɜːrfluəs]

    • 释义: 多余的，累赘的 (Unnecessary)
    • 台词: “I don’t need a partner. A partner is superfluous and dangerous.”
    • 翻译: “我不需要搭档。搭档是多余的，而且危险。”

  19. compositional [ˌkɑːmpəˈzɪʃənl]

    • 释义: 构图的，创作的 (Relating to composition)
    • 台词: “Look at the compositional balance of the crime scene. This wasn’t a murder; it was an installation.”
    • 翻译: “看看犯罪现场的构图平衡。这不只是一场谋杀；这是一场装置艺术。”

  20. unerring [ʌnˈerɪŋ]

    • 释义: 万无一失的 (Always accurate)
    • 台词: “She has an unerring sense of direction. Even in the dark, she knows exactly where the exit is.”
    • 翻译: “她有着极其精准的方向感。即使在黑暗中，她也准确知道出口在哪儿。”

Phrases & Expressions (短语篇)

  1. merge into

    • 释义: 融入，合并
    • 台词: “If you want to survive, you need to merge into the crowd. Be nobody. Be invisible.”
    • 翻译: “如果你想活下来，你就得融入人群。做一个无名氏。隐形。”

  2. to a certain extent

  • 释义: 在一定程度上
  • 台词: “To a certain extent, I trust you. But I still have my hand on my gun.”
  • 翻译: “在一定程度上，我信任你。但我仍然把手按在枪上。”

  1. abound with

    • 释义: 充满
    • 台词: “The history of this family abounds with betrayal. Why should you be any different?”
    • 翻译: “这个家族的历史充满了背叛。凭什么你就该是个例外？”

  2. take up residence

    • 释义: 定居，占领
    • 台词: “The idea started to take up residence in his mind, growing until it was the only thing he could think about.”
    • 翻译: “这个念头开始在他脑海中扎根，不断滋长，直到成了他唯一能思考的事。”

  3. the path of least resistance

    • 释义: 最省力的途径
    • 台词: “Don’t just take the path of least resistance. Sometimes the hardest way is the only way out.”
    • 翻译: “别只挑省力的路走。有时候，最难的那条路才是唯一的出路。”
