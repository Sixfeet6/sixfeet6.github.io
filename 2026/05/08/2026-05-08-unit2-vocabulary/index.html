Unit 2 单词集锦：达芬奇的艺术与山水哲学

🎬 Unit 2 单词集锦 (Dialogue Edition)

Unit 2 的主题非常优美，从达芬奇的绘画技法（iExplore 1）讲到了东方山水画的哲学意境（iExplore 2）。艺术与单词的碰撞，很有意思。:)

Part 1: The Master’s Hand (iExplore 1)

  1. uncanny [ʌnˈkæni]

    • 释义: 神秘的，不可思议的 (Strange or mysterious, especially in an unsettling way)
    • 台词: “There is an uncanny resemblance between this portrait and the woman who disappeared ten years ago.”
    • 翻译: “这幅画像与十年前失踪的那个女人之间，有一种不可思议的相似感。”

  2. quattrocento [ˌkwɑːtrəˈtʃentoʊ]

    • 释义: 15世纪的意大利艺术/文学 (The 15th century as a period of Italian art)
    • 台词: “He talks about the quattrocento as if he lived through it himself, probably because he spends more time in museums than in the real world.”
    • 翻译: “他谈论起15世纪艺术时，就好像他亲自在那儿生活过一样，大概是因为他待在博物馆里的时间比在现实世界还长。”

  3. sfumato [sfuːˈmɑːtoʊ]

    • 释义: 晕涂法 (A technique of blending colors so subtly that they melt into one another)
    • 台词: “Leonardo used sfumato to create that hazy smile. It’s not just paint; it’s a controlled blur of reality.”
    • 翻译: “达芬奇用晕涂法创造了那种朦胧的微笑。那不仅是颜料，那是对现实的一种有意的模糊。”

  4. mastery [ˈmæstəri]

    • 释义: 精通，熟练 (Great skill or knowledge)
    • 台词: “True mastery doesn’t mean you never make mistakes; it means you know how to turn your mistakes into masterpieces.”
    • 翻译: “真正的精通并不意味着你从不犯错；而是意味着你知道如何将错误化为杰作。”

  5. counterbalance [ˈkaʊntərbæləns]

    • 释义: 抗衡，抵消 (A weight or force that balances another)
    • 台词: “The chaos in his life was the only counterbalance to the absolute precision of his art.”
    • 翻译: “他生活中的混乱，是他艺术中绝对精确的唯一制衡点。”

  6. painstaking [ˈpeɪnzteɪkɪŋ]

    • 释义: 刻苦的，需细心的 (Done with or employing great care and thoroughness)
    • 台词: “After years of painstaking restoration, we finally discovered the original colors hidden beneath centuries of grime.”
    • 翻译: “经过多年艰苦细致的修复，我们终于发现了隐藏在数百年污垢下的原始色彩。”

  7. elude [iˈluːd]

    • 释义: 逃避，使……迷惑 (To escape from or avoid; to fail to be grasped)
    • 台词: “The meaning of her expression continues to elude even the most brilliant art historians.”
    • 翻译: “她表情中的深意，即便是最卓越的艺术史学家也依然难以参透。”

  8. indistinct [ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋkt]

    • 释义: 模糊的，不清楚的 (Not clear or sharply defined)
    • 台词: “In the distance, the mountains were indistinct, melting into the gray sky like a half-forgotten memory.”
    • 翻译: “远处的群山模糊不清，像一段半被遗忘的记忆，消融在灰暗的天空中。”

Part 2: Landscapes and the Mind (iExplore 2)

  1. transcend [trænˈsend]

    • 释义: 超越 (To rise above or go beyond)
    • 台词: “A great landscape doesn’t just show you a mountain; it helps you transcend the noise of your own mind.”
    • 翻译: “一幅伟大的山水画不只是让你看山；它能帮你超越内心的喧嚣。”

  2. mysticism [ˈmɪstɪsɪzəm]

    • 释义: 神秘主义 (Belief that union with the Deity may be attained through contemplation)
    • 台词: “His paintings are full of mysticism. You feel as though if you stepped into the frame, you might never come back.”
    • 翻译: “他的画作充满了神秘主义。你会觉得，如果你步入画框，可能就再也回不来了。”

  3. elixir [iˈlɪksər]

    • 释义: 炼金药，长生不老药 (A magical or medicinal potion)
    • 台词: “The emperor spent his whole life searching for the elixir of immortality, only to find it in the ink of the poets.”
    • 翻译: “皇帝倾其一生寻找长生不老药，最终却在诗人的笔墨中找到了它。”

  4. indigenous [ɪnˈdʒenəs]

    • 释义: 本土的，土生土长的 (Originating or occurring naturally in a particular place)
    • 台词: “This style of ink wash is indigenous to these valleys; you won’t find anything like it elsewhere.”
    • 翻译: “这种水墨风格是这些山谷特有的；你在别处找不到类似的东西。”

  5. cornerstone [ˈkɔːrnərstoʊn]

    • 释义: 奠基石，基础 (An important quality or feature on which something is based)
    • 台词: “Integrity is the cornerstone of his philosophy. Without it, everything else he says is just wind.”
    • 翻译: “正直是他哲学的基石。没有它，他说的其他一切都只是过眼云烟。”

  6. seclusion [sɪˈkluːʒn]

    • 释义: 隐居，与世隔绝 (The state of being private and away from other people)
    • 台词: “He chose a life of seclusion in the mountains, not because he hated people, but because he loved the silence more.”
    • 翻译: “他选择在山中隐居，不是因为他讨厌人类，而是因为他更爱这份宁静。”

  7. pervade [pərˈveɪd]

    • 释义: 弥漫，遍及 (To spread through and be perceived in every part of)
    • 台词: “A sense of profound tranquility pervades the entire monastery.”
    • 翻译: “一种深沉的宁静感弥漫在整个寺院之中。”

  8. unassertive [ˌʌnəˈsɜːrtɪv]

    • 释义: 谦逊的，不爱出风头的 (Modest and shy; not having a confident personality)
    • 台词: “Water is unassertive—it flows to the lowest places—yet it eventually wears down the hardest stone.”
    • 翻译: “水是谦逊不争的——它流向最低洼的地方——然而它最终能滴穿最坚硬的石头。”

  9. literati [ˌlɪtəˈrɑːti]

    • 释义: 文人，学者 (Well-educated people who are interested in literature)
    • 台词: “The literati of the time spent their days drinking tea, painting mountains, and ignoring the collapsing empire.”
    • 翻译: “当时的文人们整日饮茶、画山，对正在崩塌的帝国视而不见。”

  10. superfluous [suːˈpɜːrfluəs]

    • 释义: 多余的，累赘的 (Unnecessary, especially through being more than enough)
    • 台词: “In a perfect sentence, every word counts. Anything more is superfluous.”
    • 翻译: “在一个完美的句子中，每个词都有其价值。多余的任何东西都是累赘。”

  11. compositional [ˌkɑːmpəˈzɪʃənl]

    • 释义: 构图的，创作的 (Relating to the way something is put together)
    • 台词: “The compositional balance of the painting directs your eye exactly where the artist wants you to look.”
    • 翻译: “画作的构图平衡会将你的视线引导至艺术家想要你看的地方。”

  12. unerring [ʌnˈerɪŋ]

    • 释义: 一贯正确的，万无一失的 (Always right or accurate)
    • 台词: “He has an unerring instinct for talent. If he says you’re good, you’re good.”
    • 翻译: “他对天才有着万无一失的直觉。如果他说你行，你就一定行。”

Phrases & Expressions (短语篇)

  1. merge into

    • 释义: 融入，合并成
    • 台词: “Watch how the blue of the river seems to merge into the gray of the mist.”
    • 翻译: “看那河水的蓝色是如何仿佛融入灰色的薄雾之中的。”

  2. to some extent

    • 释义: 在某种程度上
    • 台词: “To some extent, we are all products of our environment, but we don’t have to be prisoners of it.”
    • 翻译: “在某种程度上，我们都是环境的产物，但我们不必成为环境的囚徒。”

  3. abound with/in

    • 释义: 充满，富于
    • 台词: “These ancient forests abound with legends that even the locals are afraid to whisper.”
    • 翻译: “这些古老的森林中充满了传说，连当地人都不敢低声谈论。”

  4. take up residence

    • 释义: 定居，开始居住
    • 台词: “A ghost is said to have taken up residence in the West Wing. I haven’t seen it yet, but the tea is always cold.”
    • 翻译: “据说有个幽灵在西翼定居了。我还没见过它，但那里的茶总是凉的。”

  5. the path of least resistance

    • 释义: 最省力的途径
    • 台词: “Nature always takes the path of least resistance. Humans, on the other hand, seem to love making things difficult.”
    • 翻译: “大自然总是选择最省力的路径。相比之下，人类似乎总喜欢把事情搞复杂。”
