🎬 Words-5 单词集锦 (Part 1)

  1. competent [ˈkɑːmpɪtənt]

    • 释义: 有能力的，称职的 (Capable; sufficient)
    • 台词: “I don’t need a hero, I need someone competent enough to fix this engine before we hit the ground.”
    • 翻译: “我不需要英雄，我只需要一个能在我们坠地前修好这台引擎的靠谱伙计。”

  2. discourse [ˈdɪskɔːrs]

    • 释义: 论述，演说，交谈 (Formal discussion or conversation)
    • 台词: “Our political discourse has devolved into a shouting match, and frankly, I’ve lost my voice.”
    • 翻译: “我们的政治辩论已经沦为了一场大声咆哮的比赛，坦白说，我已经失声了。”

  3. proficient [prəˈfɪʃnt]

    • 释义: 熟练的，精通的 (Skilled; expert)
    • 台词: “He is proficient in three languages and ten different ways to disappear in a crowd.”
    • 翻译: “他精通三种语言，以及十种在人群中消失的方法。”

  4. underlie [ˌʌndərˈlaɪ]

    • 释义: 构成…的基础，引起 (To be the basis or cause of)
    • 台词: “Fear is what underlies every decision he makes, even if he calls it ‘logic’.”
    • 翻译: “恐惧是他每一个决定的底色，即便他将其称之为‘逻辑’。”

  5. indigenous [ɪnˈdɪdʒənəs]

    • 释义: 土著的，本地的 (Native; originating in a place)
    • 台词: “The indigenous people here say the mountains have a spirit. I’m starting to believe them.”
    • 翻译: “这里的原住民说山是有灵魂的。我开始相信他们了。”

  6. boast [boʊst]

    • 释义: 自夸，以拥有…而自豪 (To brag; to possess something to be proud of)
    • 台词: “This city used to boast the tallest towers in the world. Now, it only boasts the longest shadows.”
    • 翻译: “这座城市曾以拥有世界最高塔而自豪。现在，它只剩下了最长的阴影。”

  7. prominent [ˈprɑːmɪnənt]

    • 释义: 显著的，杰出的 (Important; noticeable)
    • 台词: “She was a prominent figure in the rebellion, but history tends to forget the women who lead.”
    • 翻译: “她是反抗军中的显赫人物，但历史往往会遗忘那些领导者的女性。”

  8. opt [ɑːpt]

    • 释义: 选择 (To make a choice)
    • 台词: “In a world of chaos, most people opt for security. I chose the chaos.”
    • 翻译: “在混乱的世界里，大多数人选择安稳。而我选择了混乱。”

  9. revise [rɪˈvaɪz]

    • 释义: 修改，修订 (To change or improve)
    • 台词: “I’m going to have to revise my opinion of you. You’re much more dangerous than you look.”
    • 翻译: “我得修改一下对你的评价了。你比看起来要危险得多。”

  10. brew [bruː]

    • 释义: 酿造，酝酿 (To make beer/coffee; to develop, e.g., a storm)
    • 台词: “There’s a storm brewing on the horizon, and it’s not just the weather.”
    • 翻译: “地平线上风暴正在酝酿，而且不仅仅是天气原因。”

🎬 Words-5 单词集锦 (Part 2)

  1. reap [riːp]
  • 释义: 收割，获得（报应/好处） (To harvest; to receive as a result)
  • 台词: “You reap what you sow, and today, it’s time for the harvest.”
  • 翻译: “种瓜得瓜，种豆得豆。今天，是该收割的时候了。”
  1. indulge [ɪnˈdʌldʒ]
  • 释义: 沉溺，纵容 (To allow oneself to enjoy; to pamper)
  • 台词: “I don’t have time to indulge in your little games. I have a world to save.”
  • 翻译: “我没时间陪你玩这些小游戏。我还有个世界要救。”
  1. erode [ɪˈroʊd]
  • 释义: 侵蚀，削弱 (To wear away; to weaken gradually)
  • 台词: “Time will erode even the strongest walls. What makes you think your legacy will survive?”
  • 翻译: “时间会侵蚀最坚固的墙。凭什么你觉得你的遗产能够幸存？”
  1. reinforce [ˌriːɪnˈfɔːrs]
  • 释义: 加强，增援 (To strengthen; to add support)
  • 台词: “We need to reinforce the north gate, or we’ll be overrun by dawn.”
  • 翻译: “我们需要加固北门，否则天亮前我们就会全线崩溃。”
  1. interfere [ˌɪntərˈfɪr]
  • 释义: 干涉，妨碍 (To get involved in others’ affairs; to hinder)
  • 台词: “If you interfere with my mission again, I won’t be so forgiving next time.”
  • 翻译: “如果你再敢干涉我的任务，下次我可没这么好说话了。”
  1. concede [kənˈsiːd]
  • 释义: 承认（某事属实），让步 (To admit; to surrender)
  • 台词: “I will concede that you were right about one thing: I am out of bullets.”
  • 翻译: “我承认你有一件事说对了：我没子弹了。”
  1. notorious [noʊˈtɔːriəs]
  • 释义: 臭名昭著的 (Famous for something bad)
  • 台词: “He’s a notorious liar, but in this city, that just makes him a politician.”
  • 翻译: “他是个臭名昭著的骗子，但在这一行，这说明他是个政客。”
  1. slash [slæʃ]
  • 释义: 猛砍，大幅度削减 (To cut violently; to reduce sharply)
  • 台词: “They want to slash our funding. They want to shut us down before we find the truth.”
  • 翻译: “他们想大幅削减我们的经费，想在真相大白之前就让我们闭嘴。”
  1. compensate [ˈkɑːmpənseɪt]
  • 释义: 补偿，弥补 (To make up for something)
  • 台词: “No amount of money can compensate for what you took from me.”
  • 翻译: “再多的钱也无法弥补你从我这儿夺走的东西。”
  1. surge [sɜːrdʒ]
  • 释义: 激增，涌动 (A sudden increase; to rush forward)
  • 台词: “A massive power surge just fried our navigation system. We’re flying blind!”
  • 翻译: “突如其来的强电流烧毁了我们的导航系统。我们要盲飞了！”
  1. bolster [ˈboʊlstər]
  • 释义: 支持，增强 (To support or strengthen)
  • 台词: “We need a win, something to bolster the morale of the troops.”
  • 翻译: “我们需要一场胜利，来鼓舞一下部队的士气。”
  1. dump [dʌmp]
  • 释义: 倾卸，丢弃 (To drop or get rid of; to leave someone)
  • 台词: “Dump the cargo. We’re too heavy to make the jump.”
  • 翻译: “卸掉货物！太重了，我们跳跃不过去。”
  1. delicate [ˈdelɪkət]
  • 释义: 脆弱的，精致的，微妙的 (Fragile; easily broken)
  • 台词: “It’s a delicate situation. One wrong word and the whole treaty collapses.”
  • 翻译: “现在局势很微妙。说错一句话，整个条约就会崩盘。”
  1. constitute [ˈkɑːnstɪtuːt]
  • 释义: 构成，组成 (To form; to be part of)
  • 台词: “One man does not constitute an army, but one man can start a war.”
  • 翻译: “一个人构不成一支军队，但一个人可以发动一场战争。”
  1. epidemic [ˌepɪˈdemɪk]
  • 释义: 流行病，泛滥 (A widespread disease or problem)
  • 台词: “This isn’t just a virus. It’s an epidemic of fear.”
  • 翻译: “这不只是一种病毒，这是一种名为恐惧的流行病。”
  1. prescribe [prɪˈskraɪb]
  • 释义: 开处方，规定 (To order medicine; to state a rule)
  • 台词: “The doctor prescribed rest, but my enemies don’t sleep.”
  • 翻译: “医生给我开了休息的处方，但我的敌人们从不睡觉。”
  1. gear [ɡɪr]
  • 释义: 装备，排挡 (Equipment; to adjust)
  • 台词: “Grab your gear. We move out in five minutes.”
  • 翻译: “拿上你的装备。五分钟后出发。”
  1. prerequisite [ˌpriːˈrekwəzɪt]
  • 释义: 前提，先决条件 (A required condition)
  • 台词: “Loyalty isn’t a prerequisite for this job, but it helps if you want to stay alive.”
  • 翻译: “忠诚不是这份工作的先决条件，但如果你想活命的话，它很有用。”
  1. sneak [sniːk]
  • 释义: 溜，潜行 (To move quietly and secretly)
  • 台词: “Don’t try to sneak up on me. I can hear your heart beating from a mile away.”
  • 翻译: “别想偷袭我。我在一英里外都能听到你的心跳声。”
  1. obscure [əbˈskjʊr]
  • 释义: 模糊的，晦涩的 (Unknown; hidden; hard to see)
  • 台词: “His motives remain obscure, but his actions are crystal clear.”
  • 翻译: “他的动机依然模糊不清，但他的行为却一目了然。”
  1. essence [ˈesns]
  • 释义: 本质，核心 (The basic nature; core)
  • 台词: “Speed is of the essence. If we don’t reach the border tonight, we’re dead.”
  • 翻译: “速度是关键（本质）。如果今晚到不了边境，我们就死定了。”
  1. impose [ɪmˈpoʊz]
  • 释义: 强加，征税 (To force something on others)
  • 台词: “I don’t wish to impose, but you’re sitting in my seat.”
  • 翻译: “我不想强求，但你坐在我的位子上了。”
  1. personnel [ˌpɜːrsəˈnel]
  • 释义: 全体人员，人事部门 (Employees; staff)
  • 台词: “All unauthorized personnel must evacuate the sector immediately.”
  • 翻译: “所有非授权人员必须立即撤离该区域。”
  1. combat [ˈkɑːmbæt]
  • 释义: 战斗，格斗 (Fighting; to fight)
  • 台词: “I was trained for combat, not for paperwork.”
  • 翻译: “我受的是战斗训练，不是处理文件的。”
  1. flock [flɑːk]
  • 释义: 聚集，群 (To gather; a group of birds/sheep)
  • 台词: “People will flock to a leader who promises them hope, even if it’s a lie.”
  • 翻译: “人们会涌向那个承诺给他们希望的领导者，即便那是谎言。”
  1. strain [streɪn]
  • 释义: 压力，拉紧 (Pressure; tension; to push to the limit)
  • 台词: “The engine can’t take much more of this strain. She’s going to blow!”
  • 翻译: “引擎承受不了这么大的负荷了。它要炸了！”
  1. steep [stiːp]
  • 释义: 陡峭的，急剧的 (Rising/falling sharply; expensive)
  • 台词: “It’s a steep climb to the top, and the view isn’t always worth it.”
  • 翻译: “登顶的路很陡峭，而且风景并不总是值得这一路艰辛。”
  1. presume [prɪˈzuːm]
  • 释义: 假定，推定 (To suppose; to take for granted)
  • 台词: “I presume you have a plan B? Because plan A just got shot.”
  • 翻译: “我猜你有备选方案？因为 A 方案刚才已经泡汤（中弹）了。”
  1. rear [rɪr]
  • 释义: 后部，抚养 (The back part; to raise)
  • 台词: “Check the rear view. I think we’re being followed.”
  • 翻译: “看一眼后视镜。我想我们被跟踪了。”
  1. odds [ɑːdz]
  • 释义: 可能性，几率 (Probability; chances)
  • 台词: “Never tell me the odds!”
  • 翻译: “别跟我谈什么胜算！”
  1. embed [ɪmˈbed]
  • 释义: 嵌入，植入 (To fix firmly into something)
  • 台词: “The bullet is embedded too close to the heart. I can’t get it out.”
  • 翻译: “子弹嵌得离心脏太近了。我取不出来。”
  1. tribe [traɪb]
  • 释义: 部落 (A social group)
  • 台词: “In this concrete jungle, everyone belongs to a tribe. You just found yours.”
  • 翻译: “在这个水泥丛林里，每个人都属于一个部落。你刚刚找到了你的。”
  1. aesthetic [esˈθetɪk]
  • 释义: 审美的，美感的 (Concerned with beauty)
  • 台词: “I don’t care about the aesthetic. I care if the gun fires.”
  • 翻译: “我不在乎美不美观。我在乎的是枪能不能响。”
  1. refine [rɪˈfaɪn]
  • 释义: 精炼，改善 (To improve; to purify)
  • 台词: “We need to refine the algorithm. It’s too slow to catch him.”
  • 翻译: “我们需要优化算法。它太慢了，抓不到他。”
  1. impart [ɪmˈpɑːrt]
  • 释义: 传授，赋予 (To pass on; to give)
  • 台词: “I have one final lesson to impart before you leave.”
  • 翻译: “在你离开之前，我还有最后一课要传授给你。”
  1. viable [ˈvaɪəbl]
  • 释义: 可行的，能活下去的 (Possible; workable)
  • 台词: “We need a viable exit strategy, and we need it five minutes ago.”
  • 翻译: “我们需要一个可行的撤离方案，而且五分钟前就该准备好了。”
  1. discriminate [dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪt]
  • 释义: 歧视，区别 (To treat unfairly; to recognize differences)
  • 台词: “The law shouldn’t discriminate, but the people who write it do.”
  • 翻译: “法律不该带有歧视，但写法律的人会。”
  1. undermine [ˌʌndərˈmaɪn]
  • 释义: 削弱，暗中破坏 (To weaken or damage secretly)
  • 台词: “Are you trying to undermine my authority in front of my own men?”
  • 翻译: “你是想在我的部下面前挑战（削弱）我的权威吗？”
  1. merit [ˈmerɪt]
  • 释义: 优点，功绩 (Quality of being good; worth)
  • 台词: “Your case has merit, but the judge is already in their pocket.”
  • 翻译: “你的案子本身是有道理的，但法官早就被他们收买了。”
  1. intimidate [ɪnˈtɪmɪdeɪt]
  • 释义: 恐吓，威胁 (To frighten; to threaten)
  • 台词: “You can’t intimidate someone who has nothing left to lose.”
  • 翻译: “你吓不倒一个一无所有的人。”
  1. charity [ˈtʃærəti]
  • 释义: 慈善 (Giving help; kindness)
  • 台词: “I’m not looking for charity. I’m looking for justice.”
  • 翻译: “我不是在寻求施舍。我是在寻求正义。”
  1. genre [ˈʒɑːnrə]
  • 释义: 类型，体裁 (A category of art/music/literature)
  • 台词: “I don’t like being put in a genre. I’m an original.”
  • 翻译: “我不喜欢被归类。我是独一无二的。”
  1. relentless [rɪˈlentləs]
  • 释义: 无情的，持续不断的 (Unstopping; persistent)
  • 台词: “The desert is relentless. It doesn’t care if you’re a king or a beggar.”
  • 翻译: “沙漠是无情的。它不在乎你是国王还是乞丐。”
  1. carbohydrate [ˌkɑːrboʊˈhaɪdreɪt]
  • 释义: 碳水化合物 (Sugar/starch used for energy)
  • 台词: “If we’re going to survive this hike, we need more than just carbohydrates; we need a miracle.”
  • 翻译: “如果我们要在这场徒步中挺过来，光靠碳水化合物是不够的，我们需要奇迹。”
  1. aggravate [ˈæɡrəveɪt]
  • 释义: 加重，激怒 (To make worse; to annoy)
  • 台词: “Stop talking. You’re only going to aggravate the wound.”
  • 翻译: “闭嘴。你只会让伤势加重。”
  1. retail [ˈriːteɪl]
  • 释义: 零售 (The sale of goods to the public)
  • 台词: “I spent ten years in retail. Nothing scares me anymore.”
  • 翻译: “我在零售业干了十年。现在没什么能吓到我了。”
  1. lobby [ˈlɑːbi]
  • 释义: 大厅，游说 (Entrance hall; to influence)
  • 台词: “Wait for me in the lobby. If I’m not down in ten minutes, call the police.”
  • 翻译: “在大厅等我。如果我十分钟还没下来，就报警。”
  1. transcend [trænˈsend]
  • 释义: 超越 (To go beyond; to rise above)
  • 台词: “True art should transcend time and language.”
  • 翻译: “真正的艺术应当超越时代与语言。”
  1. qualify [ˈkwɑːlɪfaɪ]
  • 释义: 限定，使具有资格 (To be eligible; to limit a statement)
  • 台词: “Does surviving a plane crash qualify as a ‘bad day’ in your book?”
  • 翻译: “在你看来，在空难中幸存算得上是‘倒霉的一天’吗？”
  1. inherent [ɪnˈhɪrənt]
  • 释义: 内在的，固有的 (Existing as a natural part)
  • 台词: “There is a level of risk inherent in every mission. We accept that.”
  • 翻译: “每一项任务都有其固有的风险。我们接受这一点。”
