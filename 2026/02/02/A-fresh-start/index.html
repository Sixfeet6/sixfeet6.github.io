A fresh start

This is bullshit. I’ve spent nearly two days wonderinghow i would be spending by one and only vacation-since it’s gonna determin whether youo can get admitted by the CuiYing academy, but ended up with doing things totaly irrelavent things from preparing for the entrance election.

If we dive deep down to it, you’ll find out that all is about the habit. You failed, because you didn’t form a habit.(or you did but it didn’t continue.) And you can possiblly win, if and only if you form it again.

So here’s my advice.

the first thing you’ll do in tomorrow morning is waking up. I case that you’d sleep over, this time, I have a back up plan for the occasion that you didn’t wake up until 8.

  • if you woke up at 7

get up immediatly. YOu may regret to get up so early, but all you need to do is telling yourself that it’s normal in high school, isn’t it?

Then you got up, go wash your face ‘cause that will fresh you up. Then you go cook for yourself. It may take you some time. Be pacient. YOu can listen to music while doing it.

Anyway, you’ll finish your meal before 8(hopefully).

  • if you woke up after 8

Then there’s two possibilities:

  1. Dad’s cooked for you
  2. he has not.

occasion 1: eat and start studying right after the breakfast.

occasion 2: go out to get yourself something to eat and be back.

In both situations, you should be back before 9, so that we can continue our work.

part II: What’s the work?

before you continue reading, I’d like you to know that this winter vacation is almost the wierdest one I’ve ever spent!

The reason? you can see, that you dont have any burden, or dreams, do you? I say this because you’re experiencing a loss of purpose. Whether you’d admmit it or not, after being humuliated by classmates from both your college and your high school, you are starting to consider yourself stupid.

But you know what? That may be true. I say so because if i say no, the following explaination will definately feel pale and weak. But if you and I admmit it, it’s no longer a burden! We don’t need to doubt if we are right or wrong about ourselves, and just face the fact, that we are not as smart as they are.

FOR NOW!!!

Yes. I don’t believe in those stupid “inspiration of intelligence” thing of my dad, It’s bullshit. But I do believe in dilligence.

These days, I always had a question: have i changed? Then it reminds me of the wonder man show I just watched this evening: When Trevor said to Simon, that oneself is not their condition, but their experiences. Everything you’ve experienced, together,defines who you really are. so have i changed? Of cource I have. I hadn’t only because I havn’t grown up.

When I was young, I used to have feelings for everything about science. But what happens now? I’m drown into something else. TV shows, short videos, and bloody hell so much bilibili videos! And I dropped reading, I quitted watch films, and I have gone lost!

There was a time, when I could just listen to music, reading a book for relax, and go to study whenever you feel it’s the right time. Why can’t you do it now? Because you’ve lost your freewill! You’ve given it to the Licheng No.2 High School, those teachers, and you pathetic phone! You can’t go back to junior school when you could spend a day wandering in the campus, and ask why and those innocent questions, and have the joy of figuring it out, can you?

So, I guess here’s the problem. And I can’t defeat it with just poems or these weak words.

So I need schedules, and I do have one for today.

It’s basically the plan I made for this vacation. Just remember these details:
