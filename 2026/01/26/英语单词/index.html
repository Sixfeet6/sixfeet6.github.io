original version

​在认知（cognitive）科学的研究中， 科学家们努力去定义（define）人类的内在（internal）逻辑。 目前学术界已经达成了一项共识（consensus）： 真诚的（genuine）态度能够有效推动（propel）任务的完成。 相反，情感的压制（repression）通常会带来额外的心理压力。 ​ 当你沉思（ponder）某位伟人的传记（biography）时， 你会发现他从不傲慢（arrogant）。 对于工作繁忙的人来说， 私人时间是一种奢侈（luxury）。 在实验室里，研究员小心地点燃（ignite）了测试材料以观察反应。 此外，这些抽象的（abstract）观点在法律诉讼或进程（proceedings）的记录中也有所体现。 最后，即使是一个简单的称赞（compliment），也能改善团队的合作氛围。

translation

In the research of cognitive science, scientists put their efforts into defining humans’ internal logics. So far, the academic area has come to a consensus: genuine attitudes can push proples’ complishment effectively. On the contrary, represion of sentiments usually brings more mental stress.

When you are pondering a biography of some figure, you would see that he is never arrogant. For those working busy, private moments are very luxury. In the lab, researchers cautiously ignites the test material to perceive the reaction. Besides, these abstract points of view are also shown in the recordings of legal appeal or proceedings. In the end, Even a simple complishment can improve the atmosphere of team work.

original translation

In the research of cognitive science, scientists strive to define human internal logic. So far, the academic community has reached a consensus: a genuine attitude effectively propels the completion of tasks. On the contrary, the repression of emotions usually brings additional psychological stress. ​ When you ponder the biography of a great figure, you will find that they are never arrogant. For those who are busy with work, private time is a luxury. In the lab, researchers cautiously ignite the test materials to observe the reaction. Besides, these abstract viewpoints are also reflected in the records of legal proceedings. Finally, even a simple compliment can improve the collaborative atmosphere of a team.
